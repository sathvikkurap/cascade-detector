# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, socioeconomic status, or any other dimension of diversity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful**: Use welcoming and inclusive language
- **Being constructive**: Provide and accept constructive criticism
- **Being collaborative**: Work together to improve the project
- **Being empathetic**: Show consideration for others' perspectives
- **Being patient**: Help newer community members learn

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation
- Discriminatory jokes and language
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Posting others' private information without consent
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Any form of discrimination based on identity
- Trolling or otherwise disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and taking appropriate action in response to instances of unacceptable behavior.

Those who do not follow the Code of Conduct may be temporarily or permanently banned from community participation at the discretion of community leaders.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by emailing [skurap@uw.edu](mailto:skurap@uw.edu). All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

We appreciate your participation in building a positive and welcoming community!
